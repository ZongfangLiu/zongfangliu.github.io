✨ About Me

Hello! I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Zhejiang University, with joint training at Westlake University, advised by Prof. Xin Yuan. Prior to my doctoral studies, I received my M.Sc. in Computer Vision from MBZUAI in 2025, advised by Prof. Kun Zhang. During that period, I got to know Dr. Guangyi Chen, whose guidance has been pivotal to my growth as a researcher. Before that, I received my B.E. in Communication Engineering from Chongxin College (Elite Class), Shandong University in 2022, advised by Prof. Pengwei Wang.

My research interests mainly lie in representation learning, machine learning, and efficiency. I am also broadly interested in other topics in AI and beyond, and I am always open to discussions and collaborations.
